# Procedures

## Requesting a session

1. Go to the free session page (booking link).
2. Fill in the form with your details and preferred time.
3. Submit the request.
4. You will receive a confirmation; our team will get in touch to confirm the session.

## Rescheduling a session

1. Log in to your account and open the dashboard.
2. Find your upcoming session.
3. Use the reschedule option to submit a new date/time preference.
4. Support will confirm the new slot when possible.

If you need help with rescheduling, contact support using the details in the business info.
