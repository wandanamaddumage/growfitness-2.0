# FAQ

## About the business

Grow Fitness offers fitness sessions. For specific programmes, pricing, and locations, check the main website and the pricing/contact links.

## Booking and sessions

- **How do I request a session?** Use the free session booking page and submit the form. See Procedures for step-by-step instructions.
- **How do I reschedule?** From your dashboard, find the session and use the reschedule option, or contact support.

## Contact and support

For questions not covered here, contact support using the email (or phone if available) listed in the business information. We respond during support hours.
